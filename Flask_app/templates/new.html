About

GoCRIC11 is India’s biggest Sports Game with an exponentially growing user base of over 2 crore sports fans. GoCRIC11 is played by using your sport knowledge and skill. You can pick your own team made up of real players for Cricket, Football, Kabaddi & NBA. Create your team within a maximum budget of 100 credits. Your team earns points based on your chosen players' performance in the real-life matches. It's time to showcase your skill and go for glory!
